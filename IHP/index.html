Self-similarity of groups, trees and fractals

May 30th to June 3rd, 2022, IHP Paris, France

The "Self-similarity in groups and group actions" conference will take place 30 May 2022 through 3 June 2022 at the Institut Henri Poincaré in Paris, France. The conference will include topics in all areas of self-similarity of groups and the spaces on which they act, including self-similar and branch groups, groups acting on Cantor sets such as Thompson-like groups, tree structures related to random walks, and many others.
This conference is part of the special trimester Groups Acting on Fractals, Hyperbolicity and Self-similarity at the Institut Henri Poincaré in Paris.
