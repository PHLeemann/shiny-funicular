Curriculum Vitæ

Research Interests:

My research interests lie in geometric and combinatorial group theory, in graph theory and in symbolic dynamic. I am particularly interested in groups acting on rooted trees and their (weakly) maximal subgroups, limits and coverings of (Schreier) graphs, rigidity phenomenon in Cayley and Schreier graphs and graphs associated to dynamical systems. For more details, here is a recent researchproject.pdf.

I did my PhD thesis under Tatiana Smirnova-Nagnibeda supervision. My thesis won the Vacheron Constantin Science Award 2017.
